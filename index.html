What is ArTecs

We are a student organization from the University of Texas at El Paso with focus on the creation of art through the use of our skills in computer science. Our main focus is to create video games to bring forth our community's and institution's names. We also focus on various other forms of digital and physical arts, such as painting/drawing, sculpture, music, and many others. If you want to see more of what we're doing, please visit our Projects section
